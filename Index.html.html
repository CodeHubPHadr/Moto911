Moto911 Motorcycle Parts and Accessories

Passion leads to success. This is the story of Bob Lu, an enthusiast from Bulacan, who started small in the scooter modification industry during the peak of two-stroke motorcycle set-ups. As the industry evolved, so did the demand for innovation. Diverse underbone customizations soon replaced the two-stroke market, ushering in a new era of show and performance modifications as well as racing.

Racing Boy (RCB) entered the Philippine market after Bob Lu attended the ASEAN GP in 2007 and met the three brothers behind Racing Boy Malaysia. By 2008, Bob was appointed the exclusive Philippine distributor for the brand. Racing Boy (RCB) became popular for offering a wide variety of stylish parts and accessories that were engineered for performance without compromising on quality.

THROUGH THE YEARS

Through the years, it has become a recognized name and part of the riders’ lifestyle. Racing teams and privateers use Racing Boy parts on their bikes and attain good results. This has prompted several big motorcycle manufacturers to tap into Racing Boy as a partner in motor sports and product testing & development. It is also evident as Racing Boy has also officially sponsored the Yamaha Factory Racing Team for the MotoGP in 2013 and continues to do so today.

BRAND AWARDS

Through decades of brand building and continual delivery of high quality products, at the end of 2011, Racing Boy won its first trophy in the “2nd Asia Success Award” organized by the International Organizing Committee, Asia Success Inc. Racing Boy was awarded as “Super Golden Product” in the category of motorcycle rim products. In early 2012, Racing Boy once again obtained an award. Racing Boy brand was recognized as “11th Asia Pacific Top Excellence Brand” by the Asia Pacific Excellence Entrepreneur Alliance, supported by the top leading international magazine, Global Business Magazine.
